Indian Constitution Quiz

(English & Marathi Questions)

Question 1
English
What is the Preamble of the Indian Constitution?
  • A) The introduction to the Constitution
  • B) The conclusion of the Constitution
  • C) The list of amendments
  • D) The schedule of the Constitution
Answer: A) The introduction to the Constitution
Explanation: The Preamble is the introductory statement that sets out the guiding purpose and principles of the Constitution.
मराठी
भारतीय संविधानाची प्रस्तावना काय आहे?
  • अ) संविधानाची ओळख
  • ब) संविधानाचा निष्कर्ष
  • क) दुरुस्त्यांची यादी
  • ड) संविधानाचे अनुसूची
उत्तर: अ) संविधानाची ओळख
स्पष्टीकरण: प्रस्तावना ही संविधानाची ओळख आहे जी संविधानाचे मार्गदर्शक उद्देश आणि तत्त्वे सांगते.
Question 2
English
How many Fundamental Rights are guaranteed by the Indian Constitution?
  • A) 5
  • B) 6
  • C) 7
  • D) 8
Answer: B) 6
Explanation: The Indian Constitution originally provided for seven Fundamental Rights, but the Right to Property was removed by the 44th Amendment in 1978, leaving six Fundamental Rights.

  • ब) 6
  • क) 7
  • ड) 8
उत्तर: ब) 6
स्पष्टीकरण: भारतीय संविधानात मूळतः सात मूलभूत अधिकार होते, परंतु 44 व्या दुरुस्तीद्वारे 1978 मध्ये मालमत्तेचा अधिकार काढून टाकण्यात आला, ज्यामुळे सहा मूलभूत अधिकार राहिले.
Question 3
English
Which article of the Indian Constitution deals with the Right to Equality?
  • A) Article 14-18
  • B) Article 19-22
  • C) Article 23-24
  • D) Article 25-28
Answer: A) Article 14-18
Explanation: Articles 14 to 18 of the Indian Constitution deal with the Right to Equality, which includes equality before law, prohibition of discrimination, equality of opportunity, abolition of untouchability, and abolition of titles.

Question 9
English
Which of the following is NOT a part of the basic structure of the Indian Constitution as per the Kesavananda Bharati case?
  • A) Supremacy of the Constitution
  • B) Federalism
  • C) Secularism
  • D) Right to Property
Answer: D) Right to Property
Explanation: In the Kesavananda Bharati case (1973), the Supreme Court held that the basic structure of the Constitution cannot be amended. The Right to Property was removed as a fundamental right by the 44th Amendment in 1978, indicating it is not part of the basic structure.

  • अ) संविधानाचे सर्वोच्चत्व
  • ब) संघराज्यवाद

  • ड) मालमत्तेचा अधिकार
उत्तर: ड) मालमत्तेचा अधिकार
स्पष्टीकरण: केशवानंद भारती खटल्यात (1973), सर्वोच्च न्यायालयाने असा निर्णय दिला की संविधानाच्या मूलभूत संरचनेत दुरुस्ती केली जाऊ शकत नाही. 44 व्या दुरुस्तीद्वारे 1978 मध्ये मालमत्तेचा अधिकार मूलभूत अधिकारातून काढून टाकण्यात आला, ज्यामुळे तो मूलभूत संरचनेचा भाग नाही.
